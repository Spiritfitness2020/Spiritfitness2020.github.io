What is Spirit Fitness Academy?

Spirit Fitness is a way of life, it is not a diet or an exercise routine to focus on for an hour a day. Spirit Fitness is about inviting healthy behaviors into your life that will become a habit and create real long-term change. There are no cookie cutter diets or fitness plans because no two women are the same. Each member receives a personal plan that is tailor made for their body, their emotional and physical history and the demands of their daily life. The goal of spirit fitness is for each client to graduate feeling that losing weight and dieting became background noise that takes up little to no energy in their life, Instead healthy joyful behaviors should become instinct enabling women to feel good in their own skin, while feeling strong and remaining strong from the inside out!

We are a community of women that are action takers, woman that area ready to sit in the driver’s seat of our lives and are dedicated to making our health a priority everyday through daily practices that create sustainable changes into our everyday life.

Diff programs we offer:

Pregnancy and Beyond

As women we have the unique gift, power, and great responsibility of bringing life into the world. Our bodies take on a new form, adapt, and become a home not only to ourselves but to our little one for nine whole months. The knowledge of how beautiful pregnancy is does not take away from the indisputable feeling that the changes our body undergoes to house our child is coupled with many challenges. Some of these physical changes are: an expanding belly, and weight gain, an enlarged uterus, morning sickness and backaches, changes in gait, posture, as well as sensory feedback, due to the numerous anatomical, physiological, and hormonal changes women experience during the nine months of pregnancy and beyond. Spirit Fitness pregnancy and beyond program is a program designed for women in their childbearing years. Whether you are prenatal and have a desire to strengthen your body in preparation for labor, or post-natal and want to regain strength, restore your core, and reach a comfortable weight goal we have a program for you!

Strong for Life

Are you a woman who has high fitness goals such as long distance running or weight training? Do you feel yourself plateauing? Or can’t manage to eat enough calories to fuel your exercise routine? Spirit fitness is here for you. I know myself after exercising on my own for years I began to feel bored or possibly that I reached my limit. Working with a coach changed my world. It opened me up to an entirely new space in my mind and body I can now accomplish things I never dreamed were possible. I want to share this with you. Whatever your dream is 1 pull-up, 10 pull ups, or to run a marathon. I know with proper coaching you can reach that goal! You do not need to exercise excessively or for an extended period to see results. Constantly changing up your exercise routine will prevent you from plateauing, a personalized routine that targets every muscle in the body will optimize the time you put in so that you see the results you want and feel strong for life !

A Pain Free Life

If you walk around with knee pain, or have trouble bending because your back is so weak you are not alone. Hundreds of women young and old call and share with me that they cannot exercise due to being in so much physical pain. It hurts me every time and fills me with deep joy every time a client gains back mobility in an area where she previously experienced major discomfort. If this is, you please don’t hesitate. You can strengthen the muscles that support your back and core relieving yourself from pain. With guidance you can strengthen the muscles around your joints and get your mobility back! “I Knew I had really bad posture, and I have always been very self-conscious about it. And since I started here, I’ve been more self-conscious about it and since I started here, I’ve been able to activate my core when I’m walking so that I am standing up straighter” Spirit Fitness Member
